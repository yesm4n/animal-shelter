Animal shelter

This is an animal shelter. Located in Prishtina & it's main purpose is sheltering or finding homes for strays.

About Us

We are a family of 4 that decided to take matters into our own hands, by creating a non-profit organisation that takes care of stray animals. Among them, dogs and cats being the most dominant on the streets of Kosovo, endangering people & starving without proper care. And so we managed to create a proper environment for up to 300 strays with help from our community and other resources, do visit us, we'll do our best to help you out!

Contact us

If you are interested in getting a pet for yourself or found a stray that needs sheltering, contact us immediately! We are more than willing to help!!
